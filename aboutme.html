About me

I qualified as a solicitor in 1975 and have worked in general private practice throughout my career.

My specialist areas are family law, claims relating to wills and inheritance, and neighbour and property disputes.

I was a partner in a long established firm for 20 years. Since leaving partnership in 2000, I have worked as a consultant and law lecturer.

I obtained a further education teachers' certificate in 1074, and I have taught law on a variety of courses, including CPD courses for lawyers.

In 1999, I became a family mediator.

In 2015, I became an accredited civil a commercial mediator.
