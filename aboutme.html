I qualified as a solicitor in 1975 and have worked in general private practice throughout my career.

My main recent areas of work have been family law, and wills and property disputes. However, I am experienced in a wide range of contentious and non-contentious work, including advocacy.

I was a partner in a long established firm for 20 years, 5 as senior partner.

Since leaving partnership in 2000, I have worked as a consultant and law lecturer.

In 1974, I obtained a further education teachers' certificate, and I have taught law on a variety of courses, including CPD courses for lawyers throughout England and Wales.

In 1999, I trained as a family mediator, and in 2015, following further training, I became an accredited civil a commercial mediator.
