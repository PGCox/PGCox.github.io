About mediation

Alternative dispute resolution (ADR) can take the form of negotiation, mediation, arbitration, neutral evaluation, and the like.

I act as an impartial third party to help the parties involved in a dispute find a mutually agreeable solution through mediation.

Mediation saves time and expense, and the anxiety and uncertainty of court proceedings.

Even if a settlement is not reached at mediation, the process will help identify the issues and the parties' requirements, and may lead to a settlement following mediation.
