My approach

For both family as well as civil/commercial mediations, I adopt the 'shuttle' mediation approach. After the introductory meeting, the parties go to separate rooms and my discussions with each party are private and confidential. I then convey each party's proposals and other information to the other party, in accordance with my instructions. The parties may be accompanied by their lawyers or a friend, if they wish.

The mediation takes place in a single day. The parties agree on the venue for the mediation and make the necessary arrangements.

I charge a fee based on the number of hours I am involved in the process. My hourly rate is £80 to £120 depending on the type and complexity of the case. Usually the parties share the cost. Typically, mediations last between 4 and 8 hours, plus 2 hours preparation time.
